About
 
The EMU-webApp is an online and offline web application for labeling, visualizing and correcting speech and derived speech data.
General usage
 
Moving the mouse over segment and event levels automatically tracks the mouse movement and calculates the nearest boundary to the current mouse position and preselects it (indicated by marking it blue). To move the currently preselected boundary hold down the {{cps.getStrRep(cps.vals.keyMappings.shift)}} key and move the mouse to the desired position. The boundary will track the mouse movement. A event is preselected by left-clicking it (indicated by marking it yellow), holding down {{cps.getStrRep(cps.vals.keyMappings.alt)}} will move the entire event.
